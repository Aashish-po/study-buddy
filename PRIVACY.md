# Privacy Policy - StudyBuddy AI

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## 1. Introduction

StudyBuddy AI ("App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and authentication credentials (OAuth login)
- **Study Data**: Topics studied, chat history, flashcards created, quiz responses, and progress tracking
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Analytics**: Interactions with the App, features used, and time spent on each screen

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Diagnostic Data**: Crash reports, performance metrics, and error logs via Sentry
- **Session Information**: Session cookies for authentication and security purposes
- **Location Data**: We do not collect precise location data. General location may be inferred from IP address.

### 2.3 Third-Party Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This helps us improve the user experience and identify issues.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Core Functionality**: Providing AI tutoring, generating study materials, tracking progress
- **Account Management**: Authentication, session management, account security
- **Service Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns, identifying bugs, optimizing performance
- **Communication**: Sending service-related notifications, study reminders, educational content
- **Legal Compliance**: Fulfilling legal obligations and enforcing terms of service
- **Security**: Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, protecting user accounts

We **do not** sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Data Is Stored

- **Production Database**: MySQL database hosted with industry-standard security practices
- **OAuth Provider**: Authentication data stored by your OAuth provider
- **Third-Party Services**: 
  - Sentry (Error tracking and diagnostics)
  - Firebase Analytics (Analytics and crash reporting)
  - Manus Forge API (LLM requests for tutoring responses)

### 4.2 Security Measures

- All data transmitted via HTTPS encryption
- Passwords and sensitive tokens stored securely (never in plain text)
- OAuth tokens stored in secure, encrypted storage on device
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls limiting employee data access

**Note**: While we implement reasonable security measures, no transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 OAuth Authentication

We use OAuth for secure authentication. Your OAuth provider controls your authentication data.

### 5.2 Manus Forge API

AI responses are generated through Manus Forge API. Your queries may be sent to their servers:
- [Manus Forge Privacy Policy](https://manus.tech/privacy)

### 5.3 Sentry Error Tracking

Crash reports and errors are sent to Sentry for diagnostics:
- [Sentry Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/)

### 5.4 Firebase Analytics

Usage analytics are collected by Firebase:
- [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## 6. Data Retention

- **Chat History**: Stored indefinitely unless you delete your account
- **Study Progress**: Retained for your user account duration
- **Diagnostic Data**: Sentry logs retained for 90 days, then deleted
- **Analytics Data**: Firebase data retained per Google's analytics retention policy

Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where legally required to retain.

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics and non-essential tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [support@studybuddy.ai]

### EU Users (GDPR)

If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR. We process data with a lawful basis:

- **Contract**: Data processing necessary to provide the service
- **Consent**: Analytics and non-essential tracking (you can opt-out)
- **Legal Obligation**: Compliance with law enforcement requests

### California Users (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights under CCPA. For more information or to exercise rights, contact us.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years old (or your local equivalent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such information has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

Parents or guardians who believe a child has provided information should contact us immediately.

## 9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
